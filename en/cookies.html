COOKIE POLICY

Information about cookies and similar technologies used on the website.

Last update: 8 August 2025

1. What are cookies and similar technologies

“Cookies” are small files stored on the user's device by the web browser. They enable the website to function correctly, increase security, allow personalization of some elements, and support statistics. Related technologies may also be used, such as Local Storage/Session Storage, tracking pixels, or device identifiers.

2. Controller

The controller of cookies is Patryk Bobrowski. Contact: supertrewi@gmail.com. Some cookies may originate from third parties (e.g., analytics or content hosting providers).

3. Scope of use

Cookies are used on the patrykbobrowski.pl website and in embedded elements (e.g., videos) from external providers.

4. Types of cookies

  • Necessary – essential for the website's operation and security; do not require consent.
  • Functional – remembering choices and interface preferences.
  • Analytical/statistical – traffic and performance measurement; used after consent is given.
  • Marketing – personalization of content/ads; activated only after consent.

5. Legal basis

  • for necessary cookies: Art. 173(3) of the Telecommunications Law (PL) and Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest),
  • for other categories: Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (user consent).

6. Managing consent

On the first visit, a consent banner is displayed. You can change your preferences at any time in the settings center:

Cookie settings

Changes are saved in this browser and device. You can also delete or block cookies in your browser settings (which may limit the website's functionality).

7. Retention period

  • session – deleted after closing the browser,
  • persistent – stored for the time specified by their lifetime or until manually deleted.

8. Third-party providers (examples)

  • Analytics: Google Analytics 4 (IP anonymization after consent), alternatively Plausible.
  • CDN/Security: Cloudflare (e.g., bot protection).
  • Embedded media: YouTube/Vimeo/Instagram – cookies may be triggered when playing content.
  • Form protection: reCAPTCHA/hCaptcha – active only after consent.

9. Data transfers outside the EEA

If transfers take place (e.g., to the USA), they are based on appropriate legal safeguards (e.g., standard contractual clauses) or equivalent mechanisms. Details can be found in the policies of individual providers.

10. User rights

You have the right to access, rectify, delete, restrict processing, and transfer your data, as well as to object. You can withdraw your consent at any time (this does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal).
